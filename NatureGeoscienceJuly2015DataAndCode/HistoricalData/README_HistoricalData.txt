In file Historical Data
---------------
p1804,p1835,p1886 are x-coordinates in pixels of control points measured from chart images measured from upper left hand corner of image.

q1804,q1835,q1886 are y-coordinates in pixels of control points measured from chart images measured from upper left hand corner of image (that is, y is measured down).

xyz1804,xyz1835,xyz1886 are x,y,and z values of soundings from historical charts (x,y are pixel coordinates measured from upper left hand corner (that is, y is measured down). z-values are soundings in original units(1804 brazes, 1835 fathoms, 1886 meters).

z1804m,z1835m,z1886m are values of soundings converted to meters.
